Apple Inc.

Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California, in Silicon Valley.

CEO: Tim Cook

Timothy Donald Cook is an American business executive who became the chief executive officer of Apple Inc. in 2011.

Microsoft

Microsoft Corporation is an American multinational technology corporation headquartered in Redmond, Washington, United States.

CEO: Satya Nadella

Satya Narayana Nadella is an Indian-American business executive. He is the executive chairman and CEO of Microsoft, succeeding Steve Ballmer in 2014 as CEO and John W. Thompson in 2021 as chairman.

Google

Google LLC is an American multinational technology company located in Menlo Park, California, United States.

CEO: Sundar Pichai

Pichai Sundararajan, better known as Sundar Pichai, is an Indian-born American business executive. He is the chief executive officer of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google. Pichai began his career as a materials engineer.

Walmart

Walmart is an American multinational retail corporation headquartered in Bentonville, Arkansas.

CEO: Doug McMillon

Carl Douglas McMillon is an American businessman, and the president and chief executive officer of Walmart Inc. He sits on the retailer's board of directors. Having first joined the company as a summer associate in high school, he became the company's fifth CEO in 2014.

Mastercard

Mastercard Inc. is the second-largest payment-technology corporation located in Purchase, Harrison, New York, United States.

CEO: Michael Miebach

Michael Miebach is chief executive officer of Mastercard and a member of the company's board of directors. He is leading the company into a more digital world where Mastercard powers economies and empowers people by harnessing innovation and the continued application of new technologies.

Coca Cola

The Coca-Cola Company is an American multinational corporation located in Atlanta, Georgia, U.S.

CEO: James Quincey

James Robert B. Quincey is a British businessman based in the United States. After starting his career at Bain & Co, he joined The Coca-Cola Company in 1996 and was later named chief operating officer. He is now the chairman and chief executive officer at the company.
